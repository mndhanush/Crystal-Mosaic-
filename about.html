Discover The Studio

OUR STORY

We are keepers of light and lead. Dedicated to the ancient craft of stained glass, we pour our passion into every cut, curve, and solder.

A Legacy of Glass

Crystal Mosaic was founded in 1998 in a small, dusty garage by Master Artisan Julian Thorne. What began as a personal fascination with the windows of European cathedrals slowly transformed into a lifelong pursuit of mastering the copper foil and lead came techniques.

Over two decades, the studio has grown from a single-person operation to a collective of passionate glassmakers. We have had the honor of restoring historic pieces for local parishes and designing sprawling contemporary installations for modern architectural homes.

The Art of Craftsmanship

Traditional Methods

We reject mass production. Every pattern is drawn by hand, every piece of glass is manually scored and broken, ensuring that no two pieces are identical.

Sustainable Practice

We recycle 100% of our lead offcuts and repurpose glass scrap into beautiful mosaics, minimizing our environmental footprint in a historically toxic medium.

Uncompromising Quality

We source our raw glass sheets from the finest manufacturers globally, selecting only textures and colors that will age gracefully and catch the light perfectly.
