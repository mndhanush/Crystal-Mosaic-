A Space Built For Creation

We provide a safe, well-ventilated, and fully equipped studio environment. You don't need to bring anything except your enthusiasm.

  • Safety First

    Safety glasses, aprons, specialized cutting gloves, and fume extractors are provided for all attendees at every workstation.

  • Professional Tools

    Use the same high-end Toyo cutters, Hakko soldering irons, and Gryphon grinders that our master artisans use daily.

  • Catered Comforts

    For full-day retreats and masterclasses, we provide artisan coffee, tea, pastries, and a catered lunch from local cafes.
