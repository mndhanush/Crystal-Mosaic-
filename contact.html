Requesting an Estimate?

Please select "Repair Estimate" in the subject dropdown and use the attachment tool to upload clear, well-lit photos of the entire window, as well as close-ups of the damage. Include approximate dimensions in your message.

Finding The Studio

We are located in the heart of the Creative District. The studio entrance is located on the ground floor, fully accessible, and specifically designed for easy loading and unloading of fragile glass panels.

Parking Information

We have three dedicated loading spots directly in front of the studio doors. Free 2-hour street parking is available on Artisan Alley.

Public Transit

The studio is a 5-minute walk from the Central Arts Station (Red and Blue lines).

Shopping & Browsing
