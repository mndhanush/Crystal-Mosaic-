Made By Hand.
Built To Last.

Every suncatcher in our shop is created using the traditional copper foil method developed by Louis Comfort Tiffany. We do not use plastics or cheap resins.

  • Genuine Art Glass

    We source premium, hand-rolled cathedral and opalescent glass known for its rich textures and color longevity.

  • Lead-Free Solder

    All of our small, handled objects and suncatchers are finished with completely lead-free solder for your safety.

  • Polished Finish

    Each piece is chemically cleaned, polished, and waxed to protect the solder lines from oxidation and keep it shining.

The Perfect Artisan Gift

Stained glass suncatchers make unforgettable gifts for weddings, housewarmings, or anniversaries. Every order is securely wrapped in eco-friendly packaging and includes a care card. We can also include a handwritten note upon request.

*While we do not take full custom window commissions at this time, we do accept small color-swap requests for existing suncatcher designs.

Shipping & Policies

Secure Packaging

Glass is fragile, but our packaging is tough. We use custom-cut foam inserts to ensure your art arrives intact. In the rare event of damage during transit, we replace it immediately.

Free Domestic Shipping

Enjoy free standard shipping on all suncatcher orders over $100 within the domestic US. International shipping is calculated at checkout.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for non-custom items. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer unless the item arrived damaged.

Shop Updates & Drops

Because our items are handmade, we restock our shop in small batches. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified exactly when new suncatchers are added to the store so you don't miss out.
