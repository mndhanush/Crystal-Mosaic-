Learn The Craft

STUDIO CLASSES

From your first cut to complex soldering techniques, our hands-on workshops provide everything you need to master the art of stained glass in a safe, inspiring environment.

The Studio Experience

All Materials Provided

We provide professional-grade cutters, grinders, soldering irons, safety equipment, and an extensive library of premium colored glass for you to use.

Intimate Class Sizes

To ensure safety and dedicated 1-on-1 instruction, we cap our classes at a maximum of 6 students per session. You'll never feel left behind.

Expert Instructors

Learn directly from working artisans who restore historical cathedral windows and build custom commissions daily.

Curriculum Levels

Find the right starting point for your creative journey. No prior art experience is required for our Level 1 foundation courses.

L1

Introduction to Copper Foil

Perfect for complete beginners. You will learn the Tiffany method: pattern making, scoring and breaking glass safely, grinding edges, wrapping in copper foil, and basic flat soldering to complete a small suncatcher.

No Experience Needed 3 Hour Session
L2

Intermediate Panel Design

Expand your skills by tackling larger, multi-piece flat panels. This course introduces glass selection theory (light transmission & texture), precise edge fitting, framing with zinc border came, and applying patina to solder lines.

Prerequisite: Level 1 4-Week Course
L3

Traditional Lead Came

Learn the ancient architectural method used in cathedrals. You will construct a geometric panel using lead came channels, learn to stretch and cut lead, master joint soldering, and properly cement and weatherproof the final piece.

Prerequisite: Level 2 6-Week Course
