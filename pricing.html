Restoration Estimates

Restoration pricing varies wildly based on condition. We offer free visual estimates based on your photographs, followed by a formal quote once the panel is inspected in person.

Drop-in Repair

Starting at $150

Replacing 1 to 3 easily accessible broken pieces on the edge of a panel without needing to fully dismantle the window.

Partial Re-lead

$100 - $180 / sq. ft.

Dismantling a specific bowed section of a window, cleaning the glass, and rebuilding with fresh lead while leaving the stable portions intact.

Full Conservation

$250 - $400+ / sq. ft.

Complete dismantling, chemical cleaning, custom sourcing antique glass replacements, full re-leading, and historical cementing.
